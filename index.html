FAQ

How do I subscribe?

Sign up via our website or mobile app. Well contact you with a join code to enter in our app to start subscribing.

I've subscribed! Do you deliver the next day?

Yes! Our courier service delivers straight to your doorstep, the next working day! Schedule for next-day deliveries on Monday - Saturday between 12PM to 10PM. Alternatively, schedule for a pick-up at our office, just steps away from Chinatown MRT, available on weekdays between 12PM to 10PM. For subsequent deliveries, return your old tote for the new at the same time by booking for our MadSwap delivery service via the app.

What is MadSwap?

Fancy a change of clothes? Select another 3 items on our app and well come by the next working day to swap what you have on hand for the 3 new pieces. Well exchange your old tote for the new one simultaneously! No wait time in between, making it fuss-free for you busy ladies.

How often can I get an awesome new tote?

You get up to 4 MadSwaps per month - which means you enjoy up to 12 clothing styles :)

How do I know if the clothes fit? What is the measuring tape for?

In every product page, we have carefully detailed the clothing fit, fabric type, stretchability and measurements for your easy reference. In your first tote, we'll send you a measuring tape, so you can key in your vital measurements on our app and compare this against the clothing measurements. Still unsure? WhatsApp or call us and well get right back to you with recommendations, pronto.

Are the clothes clean?

Yes! We deliver em sparkling fresh. No germs, please. Our close partnership with *honestbee laundry* ensures quality, professional dry-cleaning standards. The clothes are inspected to be in like-new condition before they are sent out.

Why do you alter some of your clothes?

Most Asian women are quite petite, so we've altered the length of some of our maxi dresses and jumpsuits that run longer (and sometimes too loose) to fit the average Asian lady.

Are my credit card details safe?

Absolutely! We use Stripe, a trusted global online payment processor. None of your credit card data will be stored on our servers. All transactions are SSL (Secure Socket Layer) protected. Stripe is also a PCI Service Provider Level 1, which is the highest grade of payment processing security. You can also cancel your subscription at any time by dropping us a message.

Why rent and not just buy?

A subscription to fashion is a great way to experiment with new styles and a budget-friendly way to build your wardrobe. Its an entirely efficient way to shop.. And is also Earth-friendly! The clothing industry is the second largest polluter in the world, second only to oil. Millions of tonnes of clothes go into landfills or are incinerated every year when we use and toss what weve only worn once or twice. Youre making an actionable step to saving our Earth when you choose to be part of this sharing economy.
