Help Centre

Looking for answers? You’ve come to the right place.

General

What is MadThread?

MadThread is an online subscription-based clothing rental service. Ever dreamed about an endless closet packed with curated designer brands? It’s real. Except it takes up no space and does all your dry cleaning. Whether you need a sequined day-to-night dress for work or a classic blue number that shouts boardroom chic, we let you experiment with different looks without breaking the bank.

Our mission?

To always deliver happiness and empower every woman through the self-confidence fashion brings. We strive to bring in more styles you love and to consume fashion sustainably!

Subscription

Is there a subscription fee?

Yes. Our basic monthly subscription fee is $149/month. This includes free dry-cleaning and next day deliveries. You can cancel anytime.

How do I subscribe?

Sign up via our website or mobile app. Once a spot frees up, we’ll contact you with a join code that you can enter in our app to start subscribing.

How does MadThread work?

On our mobile app, browse the MadThread wardrobe of curated designer wear. Select 3 available items you want delivered. We personally deliver the items right to your door step on the next working day in a convenient tote bag, professionally dry-cleaned, pressed and ready to wear - at no extra cost!

Feel like a change of clothes? Select another 3 items, schedule for a MadSwap delivery and we’ll come by the next working day to swap the pieces you have on hand for the new 3 pieces. This means we’ll pick up your current tote, in exchange for your next tote, with NO waiting time in between! You get 4 MadSwaps per month - potentially 12 clothing styles.

Why do you have a waitlist?

We want to provide the best experience for every customer by ensuring that there are sufficient styles to choose from and that all deliveries are promptly met. We are constantly working to expand our collection of styles and optimizing the delivery and return processes for you. Keep a look out for a join code in your inbox!

How often can I get an awesome new tote?

You get up to 4 MadSwaps per month - which means you enjoy up to 12 clothing styles!

Can I rent the same thing twice?

If the item is available, you can rent it as many times as you want! But why not experiment with something new with every tote? You only live once!

Maintenance & Damages

I received a damaged item, what should I do?

Please WhatsApp us with your order number and images of the item as soon as possible.

What happens if I stain or damage the item?

WhatsApp us with images of the item, along with your order number. Upon return of your tote, our professional dry-cleaners are equipped to handle most stains and recommend that you do not attempt to remove the stains yourself.

In the event that additional repairs need to be done to the item, repair costs may have to be charged to your account.

In the unfortunate event that the damage is irreparable, we will have to charge you the full retail price of the item. This allows us to ensure all clothes in our collection are in perfect condition for all our subscribers. Please take care of our goodies!

What happens if I misplace an item?

Leave it with us! We partner closely with professional dry-cleaners to ensure that each piece is delivered to you sparkling fresh!

Do I have to wash the items before returning them?

Please WhatsApp us with your order number and name the item. We hate to have to do this but the full retail price of the item will have to be charged to you.

Will the items need to be ironed before wear?

All items will come steamed and pressed before delivery. However, some delicate items may experience wrinkling during the delivery process. You can either use a garment steamer or an iron on low heat. Please refer to the care instructions on the label tags on the clothes for more information.

Can I alter the item?

No, we do not allow alterations on any of our items. Most of the lengths have been altered to fit the average Asian height. For more information, you can refer to the measurement details of the specific item. If need be, you can use Hollywood tape as a temporary solution, provided you remove it prior to returning the item.

Payment

What are the payment methods?

Visa, Mastercard and Amex credit and debit cards are all accepted. If you do encounter difficulties, give us a holla and we’ll get back to you pronto.

Is my card information safe?

Absolutely! We use Stripe, a trusted global online payment processor. None of your credit card data will be stored on our servers. All transactions are SSL (Secure Socket Layer) protected. Stripe is also a PCI Service Provider Level 1, which is the highest grade of payment processing security. They handle billions of dollars worth of transactions around the world every year. Your information is safe! You can also cancel your subscription at any time by dropping us a message.

How am I being billed for my subscription?

Your monthly billing cycle begins as soon as you enter your join code and credit card details. Automatic payment occurs on the date you subscribed on, for every subsequent month.

Styles & Sizing

Why do you carry only UK Sizes 6 and 8?

We’d serve everyone if we could! As a startup with limited resources, we’re focussing on a wide breadth of styles for certain sizes first, before expanding.

How are items categorised?

On the app, you can filter through the selection according to Brand, Type, Retail Price, Size, Colour, Occasion, Sleeve and Neckline.

How do I know if the clothes fit?

We explain clothing fit, fabric type, stretchability and measurements on every product page in the app. In your first tote, we send you a measuring tape so you can key in your vital statistics and compare this against the clothing measurements. Still unsure? WhatsApp or call us and we’ll get right back to you with recommendations, pronto.

Why do you need my measurements?

Having your measurements allows you to find the best fit possible. As sizing may differ from brand to brand, we do our best to provide actual measurements of each clothing piece which you can then compare against your own vital statistics.

Is there a way I can try on the clothes before subscribing?

Yes, you may! By appointment only and at least one week in advance. Let us know if you want to drop by. We’re working hard to welcome as many of you as possible to the mad wardrobe!

Delivery & Returns

What delivery methods are available?

Schedule for next-day deliveries on Monday - Saturday between 12-10PM. Alternatively, you can opt for self-collection at our office, located at 64A Pagoda Street, just steps away from Chinatown MRT! Self-collection is available from 12-10PM the next working day.

Is delivery chargeable?

No, delivery is included in your subscription fee.

Where do you deliver?

We presently deliver islandwide in Singapore!

Do you deliver internationally?

We currently only deliver within Singapore.

I want to keep this piece for longer! Can I do a partial return?

Unfortunately, no. We currently do not facilitate partial returns. Please return all 3 pieces to be able to get 3 fresh new pieces, ready-to-wear!

How do I schedule a tote return?

Go to your Tote, select your next 3 items and click on Send To Me.

How long would you take to process my return?

Woot! There is no waiting time between totes, as you’ll get a new one in exchange for your old tote!

Can I reschedule a delivery?

No, sorry! Once your order is submitted, our tech system doesn’t allow us to rejig the delivery method/timing. However, we will try our best to help you with this, so reach out to us via WhatsApp.

Where can I find my order number?

Your order number will be displayed on a tag wrapped around your tote handle. Please refer to this order number when communicating with us via WhatsApp.

Cancellation Policy

How do I unsubscribe?

We’d hate to see you go! To unsubscribe, WhatsApp or email us us and we will get back to you within the working day. We recommend that you schedule your returns at least 5 working days before the end of your billing cycle so that we can end your subscription before the next billing date. Unfortunately, we are unable to pro-rate the monthly subscription fees should you choose to unsubscribe.
