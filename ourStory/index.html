“

Dresses sure do make women happy! We search for that “Cinderella” moment — where donning the perfect dress keeps you smiling all day… But there must be an easier way for time-strapped women to shop. What could make it hassle-free? “

MadThread was born in this moment. Fashion subscription is rife in the USA, so why not Asia? What if every woman had access to fashion from around the world, without needing to pay a fortune for it?

With two trusty interns, Nicole started serving private customers face-to-face with a small collection of styles from her favourite brands. These women had new dresses sent to them, which they swapped for more dresses. Then they swapped again. A revolving wardrobe.

A few months later, we’ve launched our mobile app! We’re a small, passionate team of individuals looking to provide you the best service ever! We’re here to deliver happiness. We can’t wait to spread joy through our dresses!
